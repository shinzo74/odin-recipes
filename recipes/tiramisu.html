Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu , from tirami su, pick me up is a coffee-flavoured Italian dessert. It is made of ladyfingers (savoiardi) dipped in coffee, layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese, flavoured with cocoa.

Ingridients:

  • 6 large egg yolk
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 pound mascarpone chesse
  • 1 cup of coffe espresso
  • 2 package of ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tbs of unsweteened cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Step 1

    Whisk together egg yolks and sugar in a medium saucepan until well blended. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture comes to a boil.

  2. Step 2

    Boil gently for 1 minute, then remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly.
